About College

Sri Ramakrishna Mission Vidyalaya College of Arts and Science is one of the Vidyalaya institutions. It was started in 1964 and has been doing useful work in the field of Higher Education. In recognition of its services to Higher Education, the University Grants Commission, granted Autonomous Status to this college in July 1981. The College is affiliated to the Bharathiar University, Coimbatore and re-accredited by NAAC with 'A+' grade.
71 place was secured by our college in the National Institutional Ranking Frame Work conducted by MHRD, Government of India for the Academic Year 2022-2023.
